# Viewflow Permission Review

Use this checklist for any workflow involving approvals, ownership, roles, or assignment.

## Checklist

- Who can start the workflow?
- Who can view a process?
- Who can view each task?
- Who can assign or claim a task?
- Who can complete a task?
- Who can approve?
- Who can reject?
- Who can cancel?
- Who can reopen or rework?
- Are ownership and role rules explicit?
- Are unauthorized access attempts tested?
- Is the audit trail sufficient?

## Review Notes

- UI visibility is not enough; server-side permission checks are required.
- Assignment rules should be tested separately from completion rules.
- Approval authority should be clear and auditable.
